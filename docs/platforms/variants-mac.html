For the most part, you'll have to implement your own locale variants for MacOS. Please let me know if you have one to share!

As mentioned on the main page, the Big Bag for Karabinier Elements by ProfXWing is a good place to start.

Also, the Kanata and KMonad key remapping tools may be used for remapping your system layout, including its locale keys.

There's a Rulemak implementation for Ukulele, by Drakk (Dreak).

Other phonetic implementations may be done by key remapping as mentioned above, or by looking at the files in this Rulemak repo.
